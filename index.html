The Legendary Rick Roll

What is a Rick Roll?

A Rick Roll is an internet prank that involves unexpectedly presenting a video or audio clip of Rick Astley's 1987 hit song "Never Gonna Give You Up". It's a classic bait-and-switch tactic that has become one of the most enduring memes in internet history.

The Birth of a Meme

The Rick Roll phenomenon began in May 2007 on 4chan's video game board. It started as a bait-and-switch trick where users were promised a trailer for the game "Grand Theft Auto IV" but were instead directed to the music video for "Never Gonna Give You Up".

Fun Fact!

Rick Astley himself has been Rick Rolled! He admitted in an interview that he fell for the prank when someone sent him a link claiming to be a video of him from his early career.

Rick Rolling Goes Mainstream

The meme quickly spread beyond 4chan, becoming a global internet sensation. It has since been featured in numerous contexts, from April Fools' Day jokes by major companies to unexpected appearances in serious news broadcasts.
